TECHBBQ VENUE LOCATION

Venue: Bella Center Copenhagen
Address: Center Boulevard 5, 2300 Copenhagen S, Denmark

Venue Details: - One of Scandinavia's largest modern event venues - State-of-the-art facilities with flexible meeting rooms - Strategically located in Ørestad district - Easy access to Copenhagen Airport and city center

×

TECHBBQ ENTRANCE LOCATION

Main Entrance Guide: - Located at the front of Bella Center Copenhagen - Clear signage directing TechBBQ attendees - Check-In and Information Desk immediately visible upon entry - Video guide available for detailed navigation (link to be provided)

×

TRANSPORTATION TO TECHBBQ

Public Transportation: - Metro: M1 line, Bella Center Metro Station - Bus: Line 30 connects Copenhagen Central Station - Train: Regional trains stop at Ørestad Station

Driving: - Take E20 motorway, exit 19 "Ørestad" - 24-hour paid parking available

Air Travel: - Copenhagen Airport (CPH) - Taxi/metro/train connections - 8 minutes from airport to venue

×

ACCESSIBILITY

Venue Accessibility Features: - Accessible entrances to all areas - Lifts and elevators to all floors - Wide, clear walkways - Nearby accessible parking

Additional Support: - Service animals welcome - Assistance available upon request - Contact for specific needs: +45 32 52 88 11

AC Hotel Bella Sky Copenhagen: - Elevators in both towers - Accessible rooms available
