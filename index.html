TECHBBQ VENUE LOCATION

Venue: Bella Center Copenhagen
Address: Center Boulevard 5, 2300 Copenhagen S, Denmark

Venue Details: - One of Scandinavia's largest modern event venues - State-of-the-art facilities with flexible meeting rooms - Strategically located in Ørestad district - Easy access to Copenhagen Airport and city center

×

TECHBBQ ENTRANCE LOCATION

Main Entrance Guide: - Located at the front of Bella Center Copenhagen - Clear signage directing TechBBQ attendees - Check-In and Information Desk immediately visible upon entry - Video guide available for detailed navigation (link to be provided)

×

TRANSPORTATION TO TECHBBQ

Public Transportation: - Metro: M1 line, Bella Center Metro Station - Bus: Line 30 connects Copenhagen Central Station - Train: Regional trains stop at Ørestad Station

Driving: - Take E20 motorway, exit 19 "Ørestad" - 24-hour paid parking available

Air Travel: - Copenhagen Airport (CPH) - Taxi/metro/train connections - 8 minutes from airport to venue

×

ACCESSIBILITY

Venue Accessibility Features: - Accessible entrances to all areas - Lifts and elevators to all floors - Wide, clear walkways - Nearby accessible parking

Additional Support: - Service animals welcome - Assistance available upon request - Contact for specific needs: +45 32 52 88 11

AC Hotel Bella Sky Copenhagen: - Elevators in both towers - Accessible rooms available

RECYCLING

Waste Management: Bella Center Copenhagen employs a comprehensive waste management system that emphasizes source separation. This approach involves sorting waste into 21 distinct categories at the point of disposal, ensuring materials are appropriately segregated before collection.

Environmental Impact: By implementing source separation, the venue effectively reduces landfill contributions to less than 0.5% and enhances recycling and upcycling efforts.

ONLINE EVENT PLATFORM

Maximize Your Experience: Our event platform is designed with an intuitive matchmaking tool and a complete overview of the program. It's the best way to connect and stay informed.

Platform Launch: Launching three weeks before TechBBQ 2025, the platform will invite ticket holders to set up profiles and start networking. It will be accessible on both desktop and smartphone.

Access: Ticket holders will receive an email with a personalized invitation and a link to download the app version. Whether you are attending in person or online, our event platform makes meaningful connections possible for everyone.

Pro Tip: Remember, the majority of meetings are arranged even before the event begins, so don't miss out!

SAFETY OVERVIEW

Our Commitment: We strive to create a safe and welcoming event for everyone by minimizing security risks through close collaboration with our security partner at Bella Center Copenhagen.

Staff Preparedness: All venue staff are trained in safety and evacuation procedures.

Security Check: All TechBBQ visitors and staff will undergo a security check at the venue entrance. Our dedicated security team will be present throughout the event to ensure safety.

Assistance: If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to approach the nearest security personnel.

×

FACILITY SAFETY & SECURITY MEASURES

Safety Features: - CCTV Surveillance - Automatic Fire Alarm & Sprinkler System - Monitored Door Alarms - 24/7 Onsite Security Officer - Automatic External Defibrillators Available - Fully Stocked First Aid Office

×

FIRST AID

Seeking Help: If first aid is needed, or if you witness or experience distressing or inappropriate behavior, please approach the nearest security personnel. They are trained in basic first aid and can contact emergency responders if necessary.

Emergency Number: The national emergency number in Denmark is 112. In an emergency:
- Call 112 - Immediately notify nearby TechBBQ staff or security personnel

×

CODE OF CONDUCT

Our Expectation: At TechBBQ, we expect everyone to respect all attendees, regardless of identity or background.

Scope: This Code of Conduct applies to all event activities, both in person and online, ensuring an inclusive experience for everyone involved.

Full Details: For comprehensive guidelines, please refer to our Code of Conduct.

×

HEALTH MEASURES

Personal Health: TechBBQ hosts thousands of visitors. If you are feeling unwell, please consider everyone's well-being and stay home. You can transfer your ticket to someone else if needed.

Hygiene: We encourage maintaining proper hand hygiene, with bathrooms available throughout the venue for handwashing.

×

PROHIBITED ITEMS

Not Allowed: - Drugs or narcotics - Weapons or firearms - Glass or deposit bottles (bring a reusable water bottle) - Professional filming equipment without prior approval - Walkie-talkies or portable speakers - Aerosol sprays (paint, deodorant, etc.) - Portable chairs of any kind - Distribution of flyers or promotional materials without permission
